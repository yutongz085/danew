天津大牛国际贸易有限公司 Tianjin DA NEW Int'l Trade

我们坐落于天津，是一家拥有多元化产品线的专业外贸公司。公司依托中国强大的制造业供应链，为全球客户提供从重型矿山配件、工业钢材到精密数控机床的高端贸易服务。

Located in Tianjin, we are a professional trade company with a diversified product line. Leveraging China's robust manufacturing supply chain, we provide premium trading services ranging from heavy mining parts and industrial steel to precision CNC machine tools.

同时，我们在电子科技（SDR开发）及专业体育器材领域也具备深厚的供应能力。大牛贸易，始终坚持质量第一，诚信共赢。

Furthermore, we possess deep supply capabilities in electronic technology (SDR development) and professional sports equipment. DANEW Trade always adheres to quality first and integrity-based win-win cooperation.
